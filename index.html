Our Story

Passion for Authentic Flavor

El Fuego Caliente was founded by the Hernandez family, who brought generations of traditional Mexican recipes from Jalisco to the heart of the city. We believe in slow-cooked meats, freshly ground spices, and ingredients sourced directly from local markets whenever possible.
